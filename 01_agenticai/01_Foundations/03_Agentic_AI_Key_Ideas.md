# Agentic AI - Key Ideas

## Tools & Actions
APIs, code execution, databases.
Turns decisions into actions.

## Memory
Short-term (conversation)
Long-term (user preferences, past tasks)

## RAG
Retrieve relevant data before answering.
Improves accuracy.

## Planning & Reasoning
Break goal into steps.
Solve complex tasks reliably.

## Human-in-the-Loop
Manual approvals for safety.

## Explainability
Show reasoning and traces.

## Observability
Logs, metrics, debugging.

## MCP
Standard protocol for tool/data communication.

## Multi-Agent
Multiple specialized agents working together.

## Guardrails
Policies and validation.

## Deployment
APIs, Docker, AWS, CI/CD.
